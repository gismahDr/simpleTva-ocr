# Accounting Safety

Use this skill whenever working with accounting, VAT, invoices, payments, reconciliations, balances, reports, or financial calculations.

## Goal

Protect financial integrity and prevent incorrect accounting results.

## Rules

Treat all financial data as critical.

Before changing code:

* Understand the accounting workflow.
* Identify affected calculations.
* Review rounding behavior.
* Review historical data impact.

After changing code:

* Verify amounts.
* Verify totals.
* Verify balances.
* Verify VAT calculations.
* Verify reports.
* Verify exports.

## Special Attention

* Bank reconciliation
* VAT declarations
* Customer invoices
* Supplier invoices
* Credit notes
* Payments
* Partial payments
* Financial reporting

## Forbidden

* Modifying financial calculations without validation.
* Changing rounding logic without testing.
* Assuming accounting behavior.

## Completion Criteria

Financial outputs remain consistent and validated.
